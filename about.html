About Us

Our Philosophy

Cacophony is a non-profit organisation where everything is available open source to New Zealand conservation projects.

We try our best to be method agnostic and know we don’t know the best way to remove pests from New Zealand. We do know a few things about learning systems and are trying to create an environment where the best solutions are more likely to pop up faster. Also as better methods develop they are more likely to propagate faster.

It is funded initially by That Quirky Foundation and PurePods Ltd. We are not seeking donations but there are a number of ways you can contribute by buying a data recorder etc. – see list here…

Given there are a how host of other organisations trying to doing a similar thing is this really worth doing? Time will tell but it seems the more approaches the better. Cacophony is very much a technology entrepreneurial approach to the problem and we believe this is a nice compliment to the other excellent community, scientific, government and commercial approaches.
